Bio

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Luxembourg, SnT, Computer Vision, Imaging and Machine Intelligence (CVI2) research group. I am conducting my PhD research under the supervision of Djamila Aouada, in close collaboration with Anis Kacem and Dimitrios Mallis. I obtained my MSc. degree from the Department of Computer Engineering at Hacettepe University. During my master's studies, I participated in research at Hacettepe University Computer Vision LAB (HUCVL) supervised by Erkut Erdem and Aykut Erdem. I received my BSc. from the Department of Mathematics at Middle East Technical University in 2018.

I am also one of the co-founders of Ray Informatics. We are a very small team of three people who build mobile applications mostly related to computational photography. Before starting my journey at Ray Informatics, I also made summer internships at IBM in 2017 (Ankara, Turkey), and Microsoft in 2015 (Istanbul, Turkey).

My main research interests are image processing, computational photography, and CAD sketches.
