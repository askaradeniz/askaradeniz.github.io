Bio

I am one of the developers and co-founders of Ray Informatics. We are making mobile and web applications mainly related to photography. I am working as an iOS and Backend Python Developer.

Currently, I am doing MSc. in the Department of Computer Engineering in Hacettepe University. I received my BSc. from the Department of Mathematics in Middle East Tecnical University.

My main research interests are machine learning, computer vision and computational photography.
