Bio

I am one of the developers and co-founders of Ray Informatics. We are making mobile and web applications mainly related to computational photography. I am working as an iOS and Backend Python developer. I also made summer internships at IBM in Ankara and Microsoft in Istanbul.

I obtained my MSc. degree from the Department of Computer Engineering at Hacettepe University. Currently, I am participating in research at Hacettepe University Computer Vision LAB (HUCVL) under the supervision of Erkut Erdem and Aykut Erdem. I received my BSc. from the Department of Mathematics at Middle East Technical University in 2018.

My main research interests are image processing, machine learning, and computational photography.
